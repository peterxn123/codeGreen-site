Welcome to CodeGreen

A hackathon by teenagers, for teenagers. Innovating towards the UK's net zero goals.

About codeGreen

codeGreen is a beginner-friendly hackathon for secondary schoolers where coders and artists of all kinds will spend 12 hours together to build a project based on our theme - tech that will help us in our nation's target of reaching net zero. Our event works to encourage participants (could be you!) to improve their coding skills and collaborate with people with all kinds of talents.

Who can participate in CodeGreen?
